ABOUT US

ISRO is Indian's space agency, operating under the Department of Space. It focuses on utilizing outer space for the benefit of India and humanity through science, engineering, and technology. ISRO playes a key role in executing the Indian Space Programme through its various centers and units.

OUR SERVICES

ISRO pioneers space exploration, satellite launches, amd technology advantages, proplling India into the forefront of the global space areana.
